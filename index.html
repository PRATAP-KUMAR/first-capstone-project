Poleramma Jatara Venkatagiri 2022

Poleramma Jatara is celebrated with grandeur. Though Kalivelamma is the village goddess, Poleramma Jatara, which is the custom of Rajas, became very famous. It's celebrated every year by Venkatagiri citizens.

2022.09.21(WED) ~ 22(THU)
@Venkatagiri, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh

Main Program

Avahana and Pratishthapan
Invoke Lord Poleramma following mantra recitation. Join both palms and recite following mantra.
Asana Samarpan
Take few flowers into your palm, fold hands and recite following mantra to offer seat to Lord Poleramma
Padyna Samarpan
Wash the feet of Lord Ganesh while chantig following Mantra: Om Siddhi-Buddhi Sahitaya Shri Mahaganadhipataye Namah
Arghya Samarpan
Offer water to Lord Poleramma while chanting following Mantra: Om Ganadhyaksha Namasteastu Grihana Karuna Kara Arghyam Cha Phala Samyuktam Gandhamalyakshatriyutam
Achamana
Achamana while chanting following Mantra: Vighnaraja Namastubhyam Tridashairabhivandita Gangodakena Devesha Kurushwachamanam Prabho Om Siddhi-Buddhi Sahitaya Shri Mahaganadhipataye Namah
